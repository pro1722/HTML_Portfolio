The Best Movies of my List

My top Three Movies

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

"Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban" follows Harry's third year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he learns about the dangerous convicted wizard Sirius Black, who has escaped from the wizarding prison of Azkaban. As Harry delves into the mystery surrounding his parents' deaths and the truth about Black, he confronts Dementors, discovers the power of his own Patronus, and uncovers unexpected allies and foes.

Avengers: Endgame

"Avengers: Endgame" follows the remaining members of the Avengers who reunites and embarks on a high-stakes mission to reverse the devastating effects of Thanos' snap, which wiped out half of all life in the universe. Faced with impossible odds and personal sacrifices, they must confront their past failures and unite to save the future of the universe.

Hunger Games Series

The "Hunger Games" series follows Katniss Everdeen as she navigates a cruel government's televised death match, the Hunger Games. Through her defiance and resilience, she emerges as a symbol of resistance, inspiring a rebellion against the oppressive regime.
